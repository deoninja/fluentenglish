Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our English tutoring services.

Your free trial lesson is a 30-minute session with one of our tutors. We'll assess your current level, discuss your goals, and show you how our teaching approach works. It's a great opportunity to experience our platform and teaching style before committing.

You can cancel or reschedule any lesson up to 12 hours before the scheduled time without penalty. Cancellations within 12 hours may be subject to a fee. We understand emergencies happen though, so please contact us if you have an unexpected situation.

All our tutors undergo a rigorous selection process including certification verification, teaching demonstration, and background checks. We only accept tutors with proven teaching experience and excellent communication skills. Less than 15% of applicants are accepted.

You'll need a computer, tablet, or smartphone with a stable internet connection, webcam, and microphone. We recommend using Chrome or Firefox browsers. Our platform works on all major devices and operating systems. Before your first lesson, we'll send you a quick tech check guide.

Most students notice improvement within 2-3 weeks of regular lessons (2-3 sessions per week). The speed of progress depends on your starting level, learning goals, and practice outside lessons. We provide regular progress reports so you can track your improvement.
